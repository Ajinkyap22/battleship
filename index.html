Our planet is attacked by aliens & is in grave danger. You are our only hope for survival. You must destroy the enemy's fleet before their super-smart CPU destroys your fleet.

  • Click on one of the coordinates on the enemy fleet to attack.
  • If it hits you'll get another turn, if it misses it will be the CPU's turn.
  • Red - Ship is hit
  • White - Attack missed
  • Grey - Ship border which you cannot hit
  • Green - Your Ship
  • Randomise - Before the battle you can re-organize you fleet.
  • Whoever destroy's all of enemy ships first, wins the battle.
